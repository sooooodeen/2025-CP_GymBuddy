About Us

At GymBuddy, we believe that everyone deserves safe, guided workouts—anytime, anywhere. We designed this system to support individuals or group at home in maintaining proper form during exercise with the support of a trainer. Even in group sessions on a commercial gym where trainers may not be able to focus on each individual, GymBuddy acts as an extra set of eyes—tracking movements and highlighting form issues that need attention.

Our system uses AI-powered pose estimation to analyze your form and give instant feedback—helping you improve technique, avoid injury, and gain confidence with every rep. By combining real-time posture analysis with visual and audio cues, GymBuddy ensures that both trainers and users stay informed and engaged.
